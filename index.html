About

As a graphic designer your head is always spinning… What can I do? How can I make it better? I love doing things with my hands, you can pass the love to each piece you make!

I’m a mother of 3 beautiful kids, 1 girl and 2 boys, and since my daughter’s first birthday party I decided to make the special occasion all the more memorable.

Why not give something useful that you can use it after the party, so I started doing bags for the girls, tooth fairy pillows, car mats for the boys… anything that make it more personal!

Another thing that I love to do is to create necklaces.

I try to make something that catch the attention, you can just be wearing a pair of jeans and t-shirt but if you add a statement necklace you can make it a showstopper! I hope you enjoy!
